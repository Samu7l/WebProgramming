Avocado Toast with poached eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of whole grain bread
  • 1 egg
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • olive oil
  • chili flakes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread to your desired crispiness
  2. In a small pot, bring water to simmer and crack in the egg to poach it (be careful to not burn it, it's only 3 to 4 minutes of preparation)
  3. Mash the avocado in a bowl and season with salt and pepper
  4. Spread the avocado on toast, place the poached egg on top and sprinkle with chili flakes and a drizzle of olive oil
